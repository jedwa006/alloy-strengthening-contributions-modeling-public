## Status legend

- **OA-publisher** — freely downloadable from the journal site, no login required.
- **OA-repository** — author manuscript on an institutional repository (HAL, KIT, etc.), legitimate green OA.
- **OA-preprint** — preprint server copy (SSRN, arXiv) of an accepted manuscript.
- **Patent-public** — public-domain US/EP patent document on Google Patents / USPTO.
- **Free-on-author-site** — informally hosted on a co-author's personal/group site; legitimate but less stable.
- **Free-on-ResearchGate** — author-uploaded ResearchGate copy. May require RG login or "request PDF."
- **Paywalled** — only behind subscription; user's institution likely the route.
- **Pre-internet/Hard-copy** — pre-DOI era, library/microfilm only.
